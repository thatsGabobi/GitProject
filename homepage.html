STREETWEAR AND STYLE FOR ALL.

INCLUSIV Apparel Co. is a by-product of a dream and vision that was once handled by Gabriel Catajoy. Founded in 2022, INCLUSIV is a streetwear fashion brand that promotes inclusivity and streetwear fashion freedom for everyone. We believe that fashion is for everyone and everyone should express their feelings and emotion through fashion. Streetwear is all about style and expression. With INCLUSIV, you are one-of-a kind. Each fashion statements and style you wear is a unique experience. We break the stigma of streetwear fashion where it is exclusive only to men and the hip-hop genre.
